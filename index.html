Information Metabolism & The 8 Functions

The foundational elements of the psyche. Rather than behavioral traits, MBTI defines the specific neurological and psychological pathways through which an individual perceives data (Irrational functions) and organizes that data into decisions (Rational functions).

Dynamic Syntax & Relationship of Symbols

To move beyond amateur profiling, we mathematically define the cognitive stack. Functions do not operate in isolation; they exist on polarized axes. Pushing one end of the axis inevitably pulls the other.

The Psychic State Equation

An individual's cognitive orientation (Ψ) is the sum of their functions (F), modified by their attitude (a : e/i) and their hierarchical weight/position (ω).

Ψ(Type) = Σ [ ω_n( F_x(a) ) ] + (Environmental Variance)

Where ω_1 (Dominant) = 4, ω_2 (Aux) = 3, ω_3 (Tert) = 2, ω_4 (Inf) = 1. Shadow functions possess negative ω weights (unconscious tension).
